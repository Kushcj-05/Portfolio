About Me

Hi, I'm Kushal, a Civil Engineering undergraduate with a strong creative drive. While my academic foundation is rooted in engineering, I’ve developed a deep passion for the design and digital media space. I specialize in graphic design, enjoy crafting visually appealing web interfaces, and love working on front-end development. I'm also a skilled video editor, with a flair for storytelling through visuals and motion. Outside of academics and design, I enjoy dancing, experimenting with cinematic shoots, and diving into filmmaking and storytelling. These hobbies fuel my creativity and help me see design from a narrative perspective. I'm always eager to learn, collaborate, and push creative boundaries—whether in engineering, media, or digital art. My goal is to build engaging, meaningful experiences that leave a lasting impact.
